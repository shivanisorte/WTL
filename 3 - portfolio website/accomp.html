Outstanding Student Branch Chair 2021 Award


Following activities were conducted by me as a student chair. I
1. Appointed operating committee heads and alloted student members to appropriate committees through proper interviews.
2. Held regular meetings of the executive committee and the operating committee heads and brainstorming sessions with the entire student branch to plan upcoming activities.
3. Attended all Student branch meetings and provided valuable feedback and advice.
4. Communicated with local student branches and Pune Section for collaboration and other necessary activities.
5. Created and preserved the records of all events held to ensure a smooth transition of information to upcoming elected officers and the orderly transfer of Student branch records.
6. Timely updated newly appointed officer and event details on vTools.
7. Conducted events and sessions. Following are the events conducted.
a) Inaugural Ceremony Of MIT ADTU IEEE STB Women in Engineering Affinity Group
b) Poster making competition with cash prizes in association with the IEEE (Topics: Marvelous women of all time/women role model, One woman many roles, Women in the workplace: breaking the glass ceiling and challenges, Respect women: End violence against women, Women in Tech)
c) Essay Writing Competition with cash prizes (Topic: If you could influence the government to take action on one issue to improve the lives of women/girls what would it be ? )
d) Research Paper Writing and Publishing Webinar organized in association with IEEE Pune Section and IEEE Computer Society, Pune Chapter, where attendees were provided with insights on the important factors that make an ordinary research paper a great one.
e) Awareness session on the benefits of getting an IEEE membership to increase the recognition of IEEE among the university students and increase membership count by making them aware of the numerous benefits of joining, with guest of honor as Akalbir Singh Chada, Chair of IEEE I2IT Student Branch.
f) Membership drive, a session held to explain the membership process to all students helping the Student Branch gain active, enthusiastic, skilled, passionate, and determined new members with uncountable ideas and suggestions for the development of the Student Branch.
g) Healthcare technology and innovation, an exhilarating session for students introducing young brains to and making them aware of the intersection between healthcare, technology, and innovation.
8. Assisted in work related to activities conducted by the sub-groups under the student branch
a) IEEE WIE MIT-ADT affinity group
b) IEEE EMBS chapter (soon to be inaugurated, first of its kind in Pune Section)
9. Stayed active throughout the year by volunteering and participating in events by other IEEE sub-organizations to motivate other student members to be more active and make the best out of their memberships. For example, participating in Eu-Reka 2021, working in the student activity committee of the IEEE Pune section, being a website team member in the IEEE Pune Student section committee, representing the student branch in the AISYWLC, and more.
